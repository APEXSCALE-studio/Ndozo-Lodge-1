About Us

A True Zambian
Home Away from Home

Nestled in the vibrant city of Lusaka, Ndozo Lodge offers an exceptional blend of African warmth and contemporary comfort. Our property spans beautifully landscaped grounds, featuring tropical gardens, sparkling swimming pools, and thoughtfully appointed guest rooms.


Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, we ensure every moment of your stay is memorable — from our freshly prepared Zambian cuisine to our attentive, friendly staff who treat every guest like family.
